    Solider




    Recruitment in the Army is a common process to meet up combat requirement. Every male and female citizen, irrespective of caste, class or religion is eligible for recruitment in the army provided he/she meets the laid down age, educational, physical and medical requirement. Recruitment is carried out on yearly basis (in two phases) by Headquarters Recruitment Unit (HQ RU)/Branch Recruitment Unit (BRU) and Training Centers.



    Eligibility


    Age.


    To apply for a Commissioned Officer you must be: 17 -21 years old on January 01 or July 1 of the year admitted and 18-22 years for those who are already serving in the Armed Forces. A Bangladeshi citizen.
    Not married.
    Having passed SSC and HSC exam both with minimum GPA-5.0 for BMA Long Course and GPA-3.5 for DSSC & BMA Special Courses. For candidates of English Medium, in 'O' level at least 04xA*/A grade and 02xB grade out of 06 subjects and in 'A' level at least 01xA grade and 01xB grade out of 02 subjects have to be obtained. A standard physical fitness as described under :


    Male

    Height :
    1.63 meter / 5ft 4 inchs
    Weight :
    50 KG (110 pound)
    Chest :
    0.76 Meter (30 Inch) Normal, 0.81 Meter (32 Inch) Expanded


    Female
    Height :
    1.60 meter / 5ft 2 inchs
    Weight :
    47 KG (104 pound)
    Chest :
    0.71 Meter (28 Inch) Normal, 0.76 Meter (30 Inch) Expanded




    Ineligibility



    . Withdrawn/Discharged from Army/Navy/ Air Force or any Government service on any ground.

    When to Apply

    Recruitment schedule is announced through national dailies on half yearly basis. The vacancies are allotted as per district. Persons interested to join army will have to register through SMS of Teletalk mobile and then apply online to get the admit card along with examination date & venue. Following documents and materials to be carried to the recruiting vanue on examination day: All educational certificates and mark sheets (Academic transcript). If photocopy then duly attested by First Class Gazatted Officer.
    Testimonial from school head with permanent address and date of birth.
    Consent certificate from parents duly attested by Chairman Union Council/Chairman Pourashava/Word Commissioner.
    Technical certificate from any government approved technical institution (for technical candidates).
    6 copies (5 cm x 4 cm) and 2 copies (2.5 cm x 2 cm) recent colour photograph duly attested by first class gazated officer.
    Necessary dress for swimming.
    Pen, pencil, scale and clipboard for written examination.
    Note : Children of serving/retired Army personnel may directly report to training centers on the day of the recruitment along with required documents. They will however only be recruited if they fulfill laid down standard.



    Selection Precedure

    Initial Screening . Initial screening includes checking of documents and preliminary medical test.
    Written Examination . Candidates qualified in the initial screening will appear written examination. The examination generally comprises of questions in Bangla, English, Mathematics, General Knowledge and IQ test.
    Final Medical Examination . Candidates qualified in written examination will appear final medical examination.
    Final Selection . Final selection will be made by the recruiting officer.
    Training Recruits will undergo training for 01 year at different centers based on their arms/services. On completion of training, recruits will be enrolled as “SAINIK” with effect from the date of joining in the centers.
